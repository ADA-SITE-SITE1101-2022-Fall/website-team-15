On the 15th of November, at 11 AM, Team 15 (Raig Abdurahmanii, Nijat Alisoy, Firengiz Bekirli, and
Nariman Mammadov) conducted the Hour of Code event at Baku School-Lyceum No.20 named after
Arif Huseynzada. The event's purpose was to show that anybody can learn the basics of programming.
The participants were 24 students between the ages of 12-15. The event began with a brief introduction
to ADA University, followed by teammates Nijat and Nariman introducing the fundamentals of coding
with "The Classic Maze" on CODE.ORG. Afterwards, we helped the students to solve the puzzles. At the
end of the event, certificates were distributed to students who finished the puzzles successfully. We,
Team 15, would like to wholeheartedly thank Ms. Chinara Kazimova, an ICT teacher at the school, for
assisting us for the whole of our project, providing us with a computer room and excellent students,
and special thanks to the Director of School No. 20, Ms. Esmira Agayeva, for allowing our group to
come and conduct our project, taking precious education time.
